About CubingZA

Speedcubing (n) - The activity of solving a variety of twisty puzzles, the most famous being the Rubik's Cube, as quickly as possible.

Cubing South Africa (CubingZA) was formed to support and promote cubing and speedcubing in South Africa. South African speedcubers first started getting together in 2012, and the first unofficial competition had just 3 competitors. Today, more 500 South Africans have recorded times in official competitions.

We are in the process of forming an official organisation to help manage this rapidly growing community. Contact us if you're interested in getting involved.


Competitions

Competitions are organised from time around the country. Competitions are held in accordance with the regulations of the World Cube Association (WCA) and overseen by South African WCA Delegates.


A list of upcoming competitions is available on the Home Page. If there's a competition near you, click the Register button, and you will be taken to the WCA website where you can register for the competition.
