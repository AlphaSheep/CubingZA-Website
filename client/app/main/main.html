About

Speedcubing (n) - The activity of solving a variety of twisty puzzles, the most famous being the Rubik's Cube, as quickly as possible. CubingZA exists to promote and encourage speedcubing in South Africa, and to assist organisers with hosting World Cube Association sanctioned competitions.
